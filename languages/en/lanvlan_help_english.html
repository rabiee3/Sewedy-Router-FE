LAN-VLAN

This page allows you to add/remove VLAN on LAN interfaces present in your box.

Adding a VLAN on LAN interface present in your system

To add a VLAN on LAN interfaced existing in system,

1. Click on the Add,

2. Select LAN interface from drop down,

3. Provide VLAN Id.

4. Click apply to apply the changes to system.

Removing a bridge from your system

To remove a VLAN from system, click on the Delete button corresponding to the VLAN.
