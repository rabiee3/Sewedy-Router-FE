Multi-Bridge

This page allows you to configure Multiple Bridges for your box.

Adding a bridge in your system

To add a new bridge in system,

1. Click on the Add button

2. Select the ports you want to add to the bridge

3. Click Apply

Modifying a bridge from your system

To modify a bridge in system,

1. Click on the Edit button corresponding to the bridge

2. De-select the ports you want to remove from the bridge

3. Select the ports you want to add to the bridge

4. Click Apply

Removing a bridge from your system

To remove a bridge from system, click on the Delete button corresponding to the bridge.
